ANSI Unit Plans

Type 'A' Units

Door clearances

Doors and doorways that are part of an accessible route shall comply with the minimum maneuvering clearances and shall include the full clear opening width of the doorway. Required door maneuvering clearances shall not include knee and toe clearance. Per ICC Section 404.2.3.

In type 'A' unit's secondary bathrooms are not required to comply with accessibility requirements per Section 1002.11.2. Door maneuvering clearances are not required on bathroom side of the door if the door swing goes into the secondary bathroom. Per ICC Section 1002.5.
